**Thought Process:**

A disco drum groove is characterized by:
- "Four on the floor" kick: Kick drum on every quarter note (every beat, i.e., 1, 5, 9, 13).
- Snare on 2 and 4: Snare drum on beats 2 and 4 (positions 5 and 13).
- Hi-hat usually in 8th notes or 16th notes, often with open hi-hat on the "and" of beat 4 or beat 2.
