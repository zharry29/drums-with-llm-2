**Rationale:**  
- Kept the original kick pattern (beats 1 & 3) unchanged.  
- Added syncopated **snare sidesticks** (`X`) on the "and" of beats 1 & 3 (16th-note positions 3 & 11) to complement the backbeat (hard snare `O` on beats 2 & 4). This creates a busier, funk-inspired groove while maintaining clarity.  
- Hihat and other instruments remain simple to avoid clutter.
